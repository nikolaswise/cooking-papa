Walnut Pesto

Ingredients

  • Olive oil
  • Walnuts
  • Garlic
  • Parmesian
  • Lemon juice
  • Basil
  • Noodles

Cooking

  1. Add walnuts, Parmesan, lemon juice, olive oil and cloves of garlic to a food processor. Blend until smooth.
  2. Add in basil, blend until smooth.
  3. Add olive oil and Parmesan until texture is right, salt and pepper to taste.
