Radichio Coleslaw

Radichio Coleslaw

Ingredients

  • radichio
  • Red Cabbage
  • Walnuts
  • Mayonaise
  • Lemon
  • Garlic
  • Parmesan
  • Mustard
  • Soy Sauce
  • Fish Sauce
  • Umeboshi

Cooking

  1. Slice and mix Radichio and Cabbage, top with sliced walnuts.
  2. Mix lemon juice and parmesan.
  3. Smash garlic, add with mayonaise to sauce.
  4. Add remaining ingredients to sauce to taste.
