Onigiri

Ingredients

  • Sushi rice
  • Canned tuna
  • Canned salmon
  • uneboshi paste
  • Chili paste
  • Soaked kombu or wakame
  • Mayo
  • Dried nori wraps
  • Soy sauce
  • Furikake

Cooking

  1. Rinse and soak the rice 30min-4hr.
  2. In a small bowl, mush salmon.
  3. In another small bowl, mix tuna, mayo, and Chile paste.
  4. In another small bowl, add soy sauce to finely shredded seaweed
