Chickpea Curry

Ingredients

  • Coconut oil
  • Onion
  • Sun dried tomatoes
  • Lemon
  • Garbanzos
  • Spinach

Cooking

  1. Dice onion, brown deeply in coconut oil
  2. Chop sundries tomato’s, zest lemon, grate ginger. Smoosh garlic, add pepper flakes. Add all onions.
  3. Deglaze pannwith chickpea water. Add chickpeas cook till golden
  4. Add spinichnand wilt down
  5. Add coconut milk, lemon juice, more grated ginger and simmer
