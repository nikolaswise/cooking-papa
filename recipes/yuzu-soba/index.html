Yuzu Soba

Ingredients

  • Yuzu
  • Wakame
  • Bonito
  • Soba Noodles
  • Soy Sauce
  • Olive Oil
  • Bok Choy
  • Scallions

Cooking

  1. Add bonito and wakame to boiling water. Simmer until broth is fragrant and tasty. This is Dashi!
  2. Zest Yuzu into a little bowl. Add olive oil.
  3. Juice Yuzu into a medium bowl. Add Dashi and a splash of soy sauce to taste.
  4. Chop Bok Choy and Scallions.
  5. Pour broth over cooked soba noodles, top with chopped vegetables and yuzu oil.
