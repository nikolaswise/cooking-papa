--- title: Yuzu Soba ingredients: - Yuzu - Wakame - Bonito - Soba Noodles - Soy Sauce - Olive Oil - Bok Choy - Scallions steps: - Add bonito and wakame to boiling water. Simmer until broth is fragrant and tasty. This is Dashi! - Zest Yuzu into a little bowl. Add olive oil. - Juice Yuzu into a medium bowl. Add Dashi and a splash of soy sauce to taste. - Chop Bok Choy and Scallions. - Pour broth over cooked soba noodles, top with chopped vegetables and yuzu oil. ---
